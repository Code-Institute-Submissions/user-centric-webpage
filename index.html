About Me

I love to learn and create new and interesting things. I am always progressive and always wanting to learn new things. Since I was young I have always been interested in technology, consoles and devices. I began to learn about programming at a young age, and would learn from different medias and online forms to increase my knowledge to use different langauges. Original began with the use of languages like Lua, and then other languages like Java, C++ and HTML. I would create simple projects within each of these languages to be able to try and improve my ability with these languages. These projects would present a range of functionality and would be made to expand my knowledge of the languages as far as I can. Since then I have began to develop my skills even further. I began within my extended studies doing a computing course, which allowed for me to be able to strengthen my knowledge of computers and programming languages. Then with the knowledge I earned from this course I also began to create and repair different consoles and devices. I would create a series of computer towers to be able to backup the knowledge I learned and actual use the knowledge within a practicle example.

Since then I have continued to increase my knowledge but during this time I would also work my time within two different industries, catering, and warehouse. These jobs would be taken for a mixture of reasons either financial or to better my knowledge. I am now a qualified chef, after completing an apprenticeship. I love to eat and try new foods, so I got a qualification so I can understand how to run a kitchen and also be able to see how a kitchen functions and whats needed to keep the kitchen functioning. Whilst I worked in the kitchen, i also did a series of jobs that would be stock monitoring, control and management, which where given to me due to overly ciritical thinking about maintaing the storage zones. I also learned about how to keep to professional standards and cook on a frontline station-based kitchen, and have spend many hours working on starters and plate-up.

Also one major part of my life has been to be a carer and to look after the people in my house and family. Its been a very wierd and strange upbringing but I can not deny how this has changed my personaliy and way of thinking. I have ranged from a series of tasks but generally most of the tasks throughout the house would be placed onto my todo. This is something that I have never tried to contest and in fact I think it has helped me be able to think more critically and make sure that once a task has been started it most be completed. This is one of the reasons I feel like I am more open minded and willing to learn whatever I must learn.
